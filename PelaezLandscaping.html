About us

Welcome to Pelaez Landscaping, where we take pride in our family-owned lawn care business. With deep roots in our local community, our family has been dedicated to providing exceptional lawn care services for over 18 years. What sets us apart is our commitment to quality, reliability, and personalized service. Today, our experienced team, continues that tradition by delivering professional lawn mowing, trimming, and maintenance services tailored to the unique needs of each client. Whether it's a sprawling estate or a cozy backyard, we treat every lawn with the same level of care and dedication. At Pelaez Landscaping, we're not just in the business of mowing lawns; we're in the business of creating lush, vibrant outdoor spaces that families can enjoy year-round. Join us in nurturing your lawn to its full potential and experience the difference that a family touch can make.
